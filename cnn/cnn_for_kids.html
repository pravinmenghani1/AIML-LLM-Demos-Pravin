👁️ Step 2: How Our Eyes Work vs Computer Eyes

Your eyes are amazing! They work in layers - first they see simple things like lines and edges, then they put those together to see shapes, and finally they recognize whole objects like faces or toys!

👶 Your Amazing Eyes

👁️ See lines first
🔍 Then see shapes
🎯 Finally see whole things
⚡ Super fast!

🤖 Computer Eyes (CNN)

📏 Find lines and edges
🔲 Combine into shapes
🎨 Recognize full pictures
🧮 Uses math magic!

🎓 Step 6: How CNNs Learn (Like Going to School)

Just like how you learn to recognize letters by practicing with lots of books, CNNs learn by looking at thousands and thousands of pictures! The more pictures they see, the better they get at guessing what's in new pictures.

CNN School Progress:

Day 1: 20% Smart
Day 10: 50% Smart
Day 100: 95% Smart! 🎉
